Variable Description
frequency average sessions per week
duration number of weeks practiced
length average session length in minutes, defined as time between start of the first exercise and ending of the last exercise of the day
cii_sessions number of sessions a student completed
cii_time total hours of practice defined as the sum of all session length
cii_readingtime total hours of reading time, defined as the sum of all exercise duration, this excludes breaks inbetween exercises
cii_words_exposures number of times the student was exposed to any word
cii_words_unique number of times the student was exposed to a new word, contain duplicates when word were presented in multiple lessons
cii_words_accurate_firsttry number of times the student read a word accurate at their first attempt, if the tutor played the audiorecording of the word the reading was regarded inaccurate
cii_words_accurate_anytry number of times the student read a word accurate at any attempt, if the tutor played the audiorecording of the word the reading was regarded inaccurate
cii_words_audioplays number of times the audiorecording of words were played
cii_words_attempts number of times the student read words a second or a third time after an incorrect reading
accuracy_firsttry percentage of words read accurately at the first attempt, defined as cii_words_accurate_firsttry/cii_words_unique
accuracy_anytry percentage of words read accurately at any attempt, defined as cii_words_accurate_anytry/cii_words_exposures
